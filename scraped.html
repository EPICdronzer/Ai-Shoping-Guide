    FAQs

    Why do petrol prices differ by city?

    Fuel prices vary due to local VAT, freight charges, and state-level taxes applied on top of the central base price revised daily by oil companies.

    How often are prices updated?

    This map fetches fresh prices every hour from publicly available retail fuel listings across India.

    Which city has the cheapest petrol today?

    Check the "Current Prices" section above — or click any marker on the map to see city-specific petrol and diesel rates.

    Are these prices official?

    Prices are sourced from publicly available retail listings and are for informational purposes only. For the exact rate, check with your local fuel station.
